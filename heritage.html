Our Heritage.

Aeronas Aerocraft represents a historic milestone in Malaysia's aerospace journey.

As the nation's first small aircraft manufacturer, we carry forward a legacy of innovation, engineering excellence, and national pride.

Our heritage is built on the foundation of Malaysian ingenuity and the vision to establish a world-class aerospace industry.

Through years of dedication, we have transformed from a bold vision into a reality that competes on the global stage.

This is our story, our journey, our heritage.

2018

Foundation

Aeronas Aerocraft was founded with a bold vision to establish Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer. The journey began with a small team of passionate aerospace engineers and a clear mission to compete on the global stage.

2019

First Design Phase

Our engineering team completed the initial design phase for our first aircraft model. We established partnerships with international certification bodies and began the rigorous process of meeting global aerospace standards.

2020

Manufacturing Facility

We established our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Malaysia, equipped with advanced aeronautical engineering capabilities. This marked a significant milestone in building national aerospace infrastructure.

2021

Prototype Development

The first prototype of our aircraft entered development. Our R&D team worked tirelessly to integrate cutting-edge technology while maintaining the highest standards of safety and performance.

2022

International Recognition

Aeronas Aerocraft gained international recognition as Malaysia's premier aerospace manufacturer. We participated in global aviation exhibitions and established our presence in the competitive aerospace market.

2023

Certification Milestone

We achieved major certification milestones, bringing us closer to commercial production. Our commitment to international standards and safety protocols was validated by leading aviation authorities.

2024

Production Readiness

Our manufacturing processes reached full production readiness. We implemented data-driven decision making and advanced manufacturing technologies to ensure efficient, reliable, and scalable production.

2025

Global Expansion

Aeronas Aerocraft continues to expand globally, establishing partnerships and delivering aircraft that meet the highest international standards. We are building a legacy for future generations of Malaysian aerospace professionals.
