HERITAGE HISTORY

Our aerospace journey from 2002 - Present

SCROLL

Dive into a journey Through our history

Evidence for our heritage is taken from different sources to reconstruct our company's state. From archives and historical records to modern documentation, we piece together the story of Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer.

The world has been experiencing a transformation in aerospace technology affecting millions of lives. Our journey represents Malaysia's commitment to innovation and engineering excellence in the global aerospace industry.

2002
FOUNDATION

Jetline Group was established

Jetline Group was established with the incorporation of Jetline Limited and PT Indonesian Airline, marking the Group's entry into airline ownership and aviation operations in Southeast Asia.

2010–2015
EXPANSION

P3TRA Capital Limited was established

P3TRA Capital Limited was established to support aviation investment, financing, and structured ownership programs.

A key milestone in our heritage — P3TRA secured SJ30 jet technology via JetLine Group, laying the groundwork for advanced aerospace development and future growth.

2015
TRANSFORMATION

P3TRA Group formally took over from Jetline Group

P3TRA Group formally took over from Jetline Group through a strategic transfer of assets, and Tri-Petra Unify Sdn Bhd was incorporated as part of the restructuring.

2016
MANUFACTURING

Acquisition of Aircraft Manufacturing Technology

P3TRA Sky Sdn Bhd, Tri-Petra, and P3TRA Capital acquired Aircraft Manufacturing Technology (AMT), strengthening the Group's aircraft design and manufacturing capabilities; P3TRA Legacy Sdn Bhd was also established.

2017
REBRANDING

P3TRA Legacy rebranded as Aeronas Sdn Bhd

P3TRA Legacy was rebranded as Aeronas Sdn Bhd, while P3TRA Infinity Sdn Bhd and Asian One Sky Group Sdn Bhd were incorporated to support diversified aviation operations.

2018 – 2022
GROWTH

Continued refinement and expansion

The Group continued refining its aviation manufacturing, advisory, and specialized aviation services while maintaining a regional and international presence.

2023
CONSOLIDATION

PETRAX5 Sdn Bhd took over from P3TRA Group

PETRAX5 Sdn Bhd took over from P3TRA Group, consolidating aviation services, helicopter operations, and aerocraft manufacturing; Heli Skytrooper Sdn Bhd, Heli Skyrider Sdn Bhd, Aeronas Aerocraft Sdn Bhd, and Aeronas Sky Sdn Bhd were incorporated under the PETRAX5 Group.

2024 – Present
FUTURE

Building the future of Malaysian aerospace

PETRAX5 Group continues to expand as an integrated aviation organization, leveraging more than two decades of experience across airline operations, charter services, MRO, advisory, consultancy, and aircraft manufacturing.
