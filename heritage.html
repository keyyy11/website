HERITAGE HISTORY

Our aerospace journey from 2018 - 2025

SCROLL

Dive into a journey Through our history

Evidence for our heritage is taken from different sources to reconstruct our company's state. From archives and historical records to modern documentation, we piece together the story of Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer.

The world has been experiencing a transformation in aerospace technology affecting millions of lives. Our journey represents Malaysia's commitment to innovation and engineering excellence in the global aerospace industry.

2018
FOUNDATION

Start of our exponential growth and innovation. Enter the aerospace revolution.

Aeronas Aerocraft was founded with a bold vision to establish Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer. The journey began with a small team of passionate aerospace engineers and a clear mission to compete on the global stage. We set out to build world-class aircraft that would make Malaysia proud.

2019
GROWTH

Engineering capabilities are steadily rising

Our engineering team completed the initial design phase for our first aircraft model. We established partnerships with international certification bodies and began the rigorous process of meeting global aerospace standards. Advanced aeronautical engineering capabilities were established, positioning us as a serious player in the aerospace industry.

2020
TECHNOLOGY

The Manufacturing Revolution aka Industrial Revolution II

We established our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Malaysia, equipped with advanced aeronautical engineering capabilities. This marked a significant milestone in building national aerospace infrastructure. Our facility represents the pinnacle of aerospace manufacturing technology in Southeast Asia.

2021
INNOVATION

Prototype development begins

The first prototype of our aircraft entered development. Our R&D team worked tirelessly to integrate cutting-edge technology while maintaining the highest standards of safety and performance. This phase marked our transition from concept to reality.

2022
RECOGNITION

International recognition and global presence

Aeronas Aerocraft gained international recognition as Malaysia's premier aerospace manufacturer. We participated in global aviation exhibitions and established our presence in the competitive aerospace market. The world began to take notice of Malaysia's aerospace capabilities.

2023
CERTIFICATION

Certification milestone achieved

We achieved major certification milestones, bringing us closer to commercial production. Our commitment to international standards and safety protocols was validated by leading aviation authorities. This certification marked our official entry into the global aerospace market.

2024
PRODUCTION

Production readiness and advanced manufacturing

Our manufacturing processes reached full production readiness. We implemented data-driven decision making and advanced manufacturing technologies to ensure efficient, reliable, and scalable production. The future of Malaysian aerospace manufacturing was now a reality.

2025
EXPANSION

Global expansion and legacy building

Aeronas Aerocraft continues to expand globally, establishing partnerships and delivering aircraft that meet the highest international standards. We are building a legacy for future generations of Malaysian aerospace professionals. The journey continues, and the future is bright.
