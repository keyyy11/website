Memorable city history

2024

Company Foundation

Aeronas Aerocraft was established as Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer, marking a historic milestone in the nation's aerospace industry. We began our journey with a clear vision to build world-class aircraft.

2025

Engineering & Development

Advanced aeronautical engineering capabilities were established, with our R&D team working on innovative aircraft designs. We began developing our first aircraft models with a focus on safety, efficiency, and performance.

2025

Continued Development

Our engineering and development efforts continue to advance, driving innovation in aerospace technology and setting new standards for Malaysian aircraft manufacturing.

2026

Manufacturing Facility

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility will be operational, equipped with advanced aerospace manufacturing technologies and quality control systems to ensure international certification standards.

2027

First Aircraft Unveiling

We will unveil our first production aircraft, representing a significant achievement in Malaysian aerospace manufacturing and demonstrating our capability to compete on a global scale.
