Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To establish Malaysia as a globally recognized leader in small aircraft manufacturing by delivering innovative, safe, and efficient aerospace solutions that meet the highest international standards. We are committed to advancing aeronautical engineering excellence while making air travel more accessible and sustainable.

Our Vision

To become the premier small aircraft manufacturer in Southeast Asia, known for our engineering innovation, manufacturing excellence, and commitment to safety. We envision a future where Malaysian-designed and built aircraft compete successfully on the global stage, contributing to the nation's aerospace industry growth and technological advancement.

Our Core Values

Innovation: We challenge conventional aerospace models and embrace cutting-edge technology to create superior aircraft solutions.

Safety First: Every decision we make prioritizes the safety and well-being of pilots, passengers, and the communities we serve.

Excellence: We maintain the highest standards in engineering, manufacturing, and customer service, ensuring quality in every aspect of our operations.

Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practices in all our business relationships and operations.
