Our Mission

Changing the country's aerospace landscape. A game changer. A Catalyst

We are committed to transforming Malaysia's aerospace industry through innovation, excellence, and strategic partnerships. Our mission drives us to be a catalyst for change, pushing boundaries and setting new standards in aircraft manufacturing.

Our Vision

Aims To Be The First Full Scale Part 23 Type Certificate Aircraft Manufacturer

Our vision is to establish Malaysia as a leading force in the global aerospace industry. We aim to be the first full-scale Part 23 Type Certificate aircraft manufacturer in the region, creating world-class aircraft that meet the highest international standards.

Our Core Values

Safety, Excellence, Innovation, Integrity, and National Pride

Safety Without Compromise: Safety guides every decision, design, and operation we undertake.

Engineering Excellence: Precision, discipline, and quality define everything we build.

Purpose-Driven Innovation: We innovate responsibly to deliver practical, high-performance solutions.

Integrity & Accountability: We operate with transparency, ethics, and full responsibility to our stakeholders.

Proudly Malaysian, Globally Competitive: Building national aerospace capability to meet international standards.
