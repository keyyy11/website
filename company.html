WHERE AEROSPACE MEETS EXCELLENCE

AERONAS Aerocraft Sdn. Bhd. is a subsidiary of PETRAX5 Group, the owner of Aircraft Manufacturing Technology (AMT) and the Type Certificate Part 23 (FAA, USA) for a twin-engine, multi-role turboprop aircraft.

As the exclusive agent and main OEM contractor, AERONAS holds full rights to design, fabricate, assemble, manufacture, market, distribute and sell AERONAS aircraft globally, while advancing Malaysia's aerospace ecosystem through technology transfer and local talent development.

Certification

CTC (Certificate of Type Compliance)

A document that shows an aircraft design meets the required safety and airworthiness standards. It proves the aircraft type design is compliant based on engineering and testing evidence.

⸻

TC (Type Certificate)

An official approval that confirms an aircraft type design is certified and accepted by an aviation authority. It allows the aircraft to be produced and operated under approved conditions.

⸻

STC (Supplemental Type Certificate)

An official approval for a major modification to an aircraft that already has a Type Certificate (TC). It covers upgrades such as avionics, systems, structure, or special mission equipment.
