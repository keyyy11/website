Company

From the very outset, Aeronas Aerocraft has been a brand for people who care about the world we live in and the people around us. We have made it our mission to make life easier, better and safer for everyone through innovative aerospace engineering and manufacturing excellence.

As Malaysia's first small aircraft manufacturer, we are committed to building a globally competitive aerospace company engineered to international certification, performance, and safety standards. Our team operates with industrial discipline across advanced aeronautical engineering, R&D, and manufacturing.

We move fast, make data-driven decisions, and challenge legacy aerospace models to deliver efficient, reliable, and commercially scalable aircraft while establishing national aerospace capability that competes on a global level.
