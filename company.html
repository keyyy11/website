WHERE AEROSPACE MEETS EXCELLENCE

AERONAS Aerocraft Sdn. Bhd. is a subsidiary of PETRAX5 Group, the owner of Aircraft Manufacturing Technology (AMT) and the Type Certificate Part 23 (FAA, USA) for a twin-engine, multi-role turboprop aircraft.

As the exclusive agent and main OEM contractor, AERONAS holds full rights to design, fabricate, assemble, manufacture, market, distribute and sell AERONAS aircraft globally, while advancing Malaysia's aerospace ecosystem through technology transfer and local talent development.

Engineering-First. Certification-Oriented. Manufacturing-Ready.

At Aeronas, every aircraft program follows a structured development philosophy:

Engineering Discipline

Design decisions are driven by analysis, validation, and operational requirements.

Certification Alignment

Regulatory compliance is embedded from the earliest stages of development, ensuring clear certification pathways.

Manufacturing Readiness

Aircraft are designed not only to fly, but to be produced consistently, safely, and at scale.

Building for the Future

Aeronas is built with a long-term perspective. We believe credibility in aviation is earned through consistency, compliance, and time.

Our commitment extends beyond individual aircraft programs—to developing sustainable manufacturing processes, nurturing engineering talent, and contributing to the broader aviation ecosystem.

A Responsible Approach to Aircraft Manufacturing

Aeronas exists to build aircraft responsibly—through disciplined engineering, regulatory alignment, and manufacturing integrity. As we grow, our focus remains unchanged: to deliver aircraft that meet the expectations of professional aviation stakeholders today and in the future
